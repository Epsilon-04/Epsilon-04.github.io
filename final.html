About


 This is our final project for CSC106. This is a madlibs game made by myself and Erich Premier.
  It is a khan adaptation of the classic game, madlibs. If you don't know, madlibs is a game where
 you get a sentence and put your own words in for nouns, verbs, adjectives, etc.
  The game is intended for young children ages 6-8 (hence the simplicity of the mad lib).
 It repeats because it is supposed to be a song.
